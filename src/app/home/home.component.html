"Abhiyuthhan" is a week long technical fest organised by the robotics club counting in engrossing guest sessions and freaky events celebrating the spirit of robotics and teamwork.

The techweek, being conducted in virtual mode, aims at providing a common platform to all the tech aficionados and showcase their skills and problem solving abilities. This is will also bring some thrill in pandemic induced monotonic life.
